Curriculum and Pathways

Building the foundation of a high-level engineer.

Scientific and Mathematics Excellence

Our curriculum emphasizes a strong foundation in scientific and mathematical principles, preparing students for advanced studies and careers in technology and engineering.

Coding Fundamentals

Students will learn the basics of programming, problem-solving techniques, and software development practices through hands-on projects and real-world applications.

Computer Architecture and Operating Systems

Students will explore the design and functionality of computer systems, including hardware components and operating system principles.

Agile Project Management

Students will learn the principles and practices of agile methodologies, enabling them to manage projects effectively in dynamic and collaborative environments.
